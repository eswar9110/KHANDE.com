Mars is known as the Red Planet because of the iron oxide, or rust, in its soil and rocks. It's one of the easiest planets to see in the night sky, appearing as a bright red point of light. Mars is the second smallest planet in our solar system, after Mercury. It's about half the size of Earth, with a diameter of 6,791 kilometers.
